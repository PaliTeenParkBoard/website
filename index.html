ABOUT THE BOARD

Our mission

Representative

We are a group of teenagers representing teenagers and parents in the Palisades Park. We have a 11 person executive board and a 20 person general advisory council that hold events often in the Park. We meet bi-monthly in the Park Gym.

Passion

We all have a passion for public service and helping the space we as a community share grow while fostering a safe and inclusive space for all, many of us are involved in other community outreach programs and have expirence in bettering our community.
